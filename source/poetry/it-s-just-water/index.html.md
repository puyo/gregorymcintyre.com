---
title: it's just water
date: 2021-07-28 14:27 AEST
---

```poem
it's raining softly like
don't mind me
just quiet rain
just water

for the silverbeet
for the parsley
for the basil
the birds

it needn't worry you
rosy cheeked babe
noisy and bright
tumbling about
please don't worry
about me
it's just water
```
