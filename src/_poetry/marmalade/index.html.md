---
title: Marmalade
date: 2020-11-05 13:20 AEDT
---

```prompt
Shaye Loudon: Marmalade. Is how we see ourselves the same as others see us?
```

```poem
i'll hide behind
my red gingham shawl
don't find my rind
with your unkind mind

seal these lips
i'm not all jelly
don't feel my peel
the bitter is real
```
